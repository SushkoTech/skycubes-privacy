Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [5 March 2025]

Thank you for playing [Sky Cubes] ("the Game"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Game.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users. The Game does not require account registration, and we do not track user activity.

Local Data Storage

The Game uses PlayerPrefs, a Unity feature, to store player settings such as sound volume, music volume, selected skin, and others. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the Game. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, which you can review here: Google Privacy Policy.

If you wish to disable personalized ads, you can do so in your device settings:
  • On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • On iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the Game or on an associated page.
